## c02-data-exploration

This component is for exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the transformed datasets.

- **Typical contents**: notebooks and scripts for summary statistics, data profiling, exploratory visualizations, and sanity checks.
- **Inputs**: analysis-ready datasets produced in `c01-data-transformation` (usually stored under `data/processed/` or similar).
- **Outputs**: insights, figures, and notes that inform the design of the final analyses (often saved under `results`).
